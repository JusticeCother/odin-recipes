Tacos

Ingredients

  • 8 (5-inch) corn tortillas
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil, or as needed for frying
  • 1/2 cup minced white onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • lime wedges, for serving
  • 1/4 cup salsa, or as needed, for serving

Instructions

  1. Lay 8 tortillas flat on a surface.
  2. Combine beef, taco seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Press 1/4 cup of beef mixture onto half of each tortilla.
  4. Heat 1/4-inch of oil in a high-sided skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Working in batches, carefully add one taco at a time, flat, to the hot oil.
  5. Hold empty side of the tortilla with a pair of tongs, and fold over beef filling once the shell is softened, about 5 seconds.
  6. Cook, turning once, until shell is golden brown and beef is no longer pink in the center, 30 seconds to 1 minute per side.. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  7. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  8. Serve immediately topped with onion and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges, salsa, and hot sauce.
