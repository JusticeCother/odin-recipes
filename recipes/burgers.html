Burger Recipe

Step by step guide to making a Mac and Cheese Burger. This recipe proves that mac & cheese isn't just for kids, check it out!

Ingredients

  • 8oz Ground Beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp meat seasoning
  • ½ cup of your favorite mac-n-cheese
  • 1/4 cup minced jalapeno
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 1 slice sharp chedder

Instructions

  1. Mince jalapeno and set aside.
  2. Begin cooking bacon. You can put it in a pan, in the oven for about 15min at 400 degrees.
  3. Get your grill/pan/etc hot & begin boliing water.
  4. Mix salt and meat seasoning with the meat
  5. Form meat with Ultimate Burger Press.
  6. Grill your burger and cook to your desired temperature.
